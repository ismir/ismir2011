CORPORATE SUPPORT/ SPONSORS

The 12th International Society of Music Information Retrieval Conference would like to offer sponsorship opportunities and exhibit space for interested organizations. Your support will be clearly visible to the key personnel in the field of Music Information Retrieval and related fields. The sponsorship is available at three different levels as indicated below.

Please contact Colby Leider (cleider@miami.edu) or Mitsu Ogihara (ogihara@cs.miami.edu) for further information.

Gold: 5,000 USD

  • Exhibition space during demonstration session
  • Large-size logo on conference banners
  • Half-page-size logo in conference program
  • Large-size, separated logo in printed proceedings
  • Two complementary conference registrations

Silver: 3,000 USD

  • Exhibition space during demonstration session
  • Medium-size logo on conference banners
  • Quarter-page-size logo in conference program
  • Medium-size, separated logo in printed proceedings
  • One complementary conference registration

Bronze: 1,000 USD

  • Exhibition space during demonstration session
  • Small-size logo on conference banners
  • Logo on the eight-page in conference program
  • Small-size logo in printed proceedings

ISMIR2011 also offers exhibition space. The fee is 350 USD.
