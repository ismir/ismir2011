HOW TO REACH MIAMI BEACH

From Miami International Airport

Taxicabs and SuperShuttle

Taxicabs and SuperShuttle (305-871-2000) vans are available on the arrival (ground) level of the airport, outside of the baggage claim areas. It costs $19 per passenger for the shuttle service from the airport to the conference venue. Taxi is $32 flat charge one way plus tips.

Cars

Car rental shuttles pick-up and drop-off areas are located on the upper (departure) level of the airport. It takes about 25 minutes driving from the airport to Miami Beach. To reach the conference venue:

  • Exit Airport Lejune Road to Airport Expressway 112
  • Follow East – will merge with I-195 (Beaches Exit)
  • Stay on left lane will exit on Arthur Godfrey Road (41st Street) – Follow East
  • Take (Left Lane) North on Indian Creek Drive
  • Will merge with Collins Avenue – proceed to 4833 Collins Avenue
  • The resort is on the right side of Collins Avenue

From Fort Lauderdale International Airport

Taxicabs and Shared Rides

GO Airport Shuttle (954-561-8888 or 800-244-8252) provides shared ride and private car services. The estimated rates are $21 per passenger for shared ride and $70 for private car service.

Taxi cabs are available at the Taxi Podium located outside the baggage area on the lower level at the curb of each terminal. The rate is approximately $72 from the airport to Miami Beach.

Cars

The Rental Car Center locates in one building next to Terminal 1. It takes about 50 minutes driving from the airport to Miami Beach. To reach the conference venue:

  • Exit Airport to I-595 West to I-95 South
  • I-95 South to I-195 Miami Beach
  • Exit Arthur Godfrey Road (41st Street) – Follow East
  • Take (Left Lane) North on Indian Creek Drive
  • Will merge with Collins Avenue – proceed to 4833 Collins Avenue
